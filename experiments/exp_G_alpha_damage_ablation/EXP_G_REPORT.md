## 5. Primary Question — Answer

**Does the Real-ESRGAN/JPEG anti-complementarity remain stable across α?**

**YES — the anti-complementarity is robust to embedding strength.**

Spearman ρ ranges from -0.716 to -0.706 across α.
The sign is consistently negative, and the absolute magnitude shows
only 0.0098 variation. The relative DCT perturbation pattern
is determined by the attack (ESRGAN vs JPEG) rather than the embedding strength.

This supports the paper claim that the ESRGAN/JPEG anti-complementarity
is a property of the attacks themselves, not an artifact of a specific α.

## 6. Interpretation Notes

- Results are correlational. No causal mechanism is claimed from these correlations alone.
- 'Stable' means: relative ranking of coefficient positions is preserved,
  not that absolute damage magnitudes are identical across α.
- Isotropy (how symmetric ESRGAN damage is) is reported per α but not claimed
  as 'intrinsic' to ESRGAN without further evidence.
